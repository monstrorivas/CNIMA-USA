About CNIMA

The CNIMA (Centre National et International de Musique et d'Accordéon) is a welcoming, internationally recognized accordion school in France, founded by esteemed teacher Jacques Mornet and award-winning accordionist Nathalie Boucheix.

Students at CNIMA have achieved remarkable success over the years, earning an impressive 123 awards in international competitions. The school has helped shape several generations of talented French accordionists.

At the heart of Mornet’s approach is the idea of treating the accordion as a wind instrument rather than a keyboard alone. When the flow of air is used with intention and paired with a sensitive touch on the keys, it opens the door to expressive playing and a truly liberated musicality.
